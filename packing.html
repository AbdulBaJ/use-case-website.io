Guided packing with barcode checks, box/padding presets, photo proof, and shipping label generation.

The Packing Workstation ensures every drone and accessory is included and protected. Operators follow a standardized flow with barcode validation, correct box/padding selection, and automated label printing—with photo proof for traceability.

How it works

Five-step packing flow
  1. Verify order — Scan the order and confirm required items.
  2. Pack items — Place drone and accessories per packing guide.
  3. Secure — Add correct padding and close the box.
  4. Label — Print/apply shipping and customs labels.
  5. Proof & stage — Capture photo proof, stage for pickup.
