Quality Drone Arm Check

Automated vision inspection and dimensional validation for 3D printed drone arms to ensure defect-free production.

See Features How it works

The Quality Drone Arm Check station verifies every printed arm for dimensional accuracy, surface integrity, and assembly readiness. High-resolution cameras and sensors detect imperfections before they reach assembly, reducing rework and waste.

How it works

Five simple steps
  1. Receive part — The printed arm is placed on the inspection fixture.
  2. Automated scanning — Cameras and sensors capture surface and dimensional data.
  3. Analysis — System compares measurements to design specifications.
  4. Pass/Fail decision — Accepted parts move to assembly; failed parts go to rework.
  5. Data upload — Results sent to MES/ERP for tracking and reporting.
