Cobot Handling

Collaborative robot transfers packed boxes to organized storage with safety sensors and flexible positioning.

See Features How it works

The Cobot Handling station uses a collaborative robotic arm to move packed and labeled boxes from the packing station to a designated storage area. With built-in sensors, the cobot ensures safe operation around people while maintaining high throughput and precise placement.

How it works

Four-step handling process
  1. Receive box — Detects incoming packed box from conveyor or packing station.
  2. Grip & lift — Uses vacuum or gripper tool to safely pick up the box.
  3. Transport — Moves box to assigned storage rack or pallet.
  4. Release & confirm — Places box precisely, logs action to MES/WMS.
