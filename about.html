About the Smart Factory – Drone Line

Industry 4.0 • Innovation • Digital Twin

The Smart Manufacturing Drone Line is a live demonstration of how Industry 4.0 technologies come together to build the future of production. It integrates robotics, 3D printing, quality control, and autonomous logistics into one connected ecosystem, showcasing the full lifecycle of drone manufacturing.

By combining advanced automation with digital tools such as Product Lifecycle Management (PLM), Manufacturing Execution System (MES), and SCADA, the line offers real-time visibility, quality assurance, predictive maintenance, and energy management. This creates a model for factories that are more efficient, flexible, and sustainable.

Beyond technology, the Drone Line serves as a Center of Excellence to train the next generation of engineers and technicians. With hands-on exposure to robotics, IIoT, additive manufacturing, and autonomous systems, it helps build the workforce that will lead Saudi Arabia’s industrial transformation.

Our vision is clear: to evolve into a fully self-sustaining Industry 4.0 hub that supports local industries, fosters innovation, and positions the Kingdom as a global leader in smart manufacturing.
