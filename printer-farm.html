How it works

Five simple steps
  1. Intake — Submit parts with material and profile selection.
  2. Scheduling — Queue assigns jobs by capacity and due date.
  3. Printing — Live monitoring; alerts on failure or completion.
  4. Post-processing — Wash/cure (resin) or cleanup (FDM) and inspect.
  5. Release — Pass parts move to Quality Check; fails reprint.
