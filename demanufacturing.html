Demanufacturing Workstation

Systematic teardown of returned or defective drones to recover components, recycle materials, and minimize waste.

See Features How it works

The Demanufacturing Workstation is designed to break down drones into reusable and recyclable parts. Each step is documented to ensure component traceability, quality verification, and compliance with sustainability standards.

How it works

Step-by-step breakdown
  1. Intake — Receive defective or returned drones from inspection.
  2. Disassembly — Remove parts systematically using dedicated tools.
  3. Sorting — Separate components into reusable, recyclable, or scrap bins.
  4. Quality check — Test reusable parts for function and reliability.
  5. Documentation — Log process and update inventory database.
